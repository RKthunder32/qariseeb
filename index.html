About Our Academy

Welcome to Al-Huda Quran Academy

We are dedicated to teaching the Holy Quran with proper Tajweed rules to students of all ages. Our mission is to make Quranic education accessible to everyone while maintaining the highest standards of teaching.

At Al-Huda Academy, we focus on three main areas: Nazra (recitation), Hifz (memorization), and teaching Masnoon Duas (prayers from Sunnah). Our curriculum is designed to help students develop a strong connection with the Quran.

We believe in personalized attention for each student, ensuring they learn at their own pace while maintaining proper pronunciation and understanding of the divine words.
