About Our Academy

Umul Qura Academy is a premier online Quran learning institution dedicated to providing authentic Islamic education with a focus on Quranic studies. Our mission is to nurture a deep connection with the Holy Quran while maintaining the highest standards of Tajweed and recitation.

Founded by a team of Al-Azhar graduates and certified Qaris, we combine traditional teaching methodologies with modern educational technology to create an immersive learning experience. Our curriculum is designed to cater to students of all ages and levels, from absolute beginners to advanced learners.

We take pride in our personalized approach, where each student receives individual attention and a customized learning plan to ensure steady progress and mastery of the Quran.
