About Us

Umul Qura Academy is dedicated to providing high-quality Islamic education with a focus on Quranic studies. Our mission is to nurture a deep connection with the Holy Quran while maintaining the highest standards of Tajweed and recitation.

We offer a comprehensive curriculum that caters to students of all ages and levels, from beginners to advanced learners. Our approach combines traditional teaching methods with modern educational techniques to ensure effective learning.

At Umul Qura Academy, we believe in creating an environment that fosters love for the Quran and Islamic values while developing strong recitation and memorization skills.
